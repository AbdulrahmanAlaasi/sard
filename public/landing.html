Stop splitting your attention in meetings.

Scrivano stays present so you can be. Speech-to-text runs in your browser — audio never leaves your machine — and your own local AI turns the conversation into notes, decisions, and tasks. Connect your own server to grow searchable, approved organizational memory.

Start a meeting — free, local, no account Self-host the backend

Local mode needs no sign-up, no cloud, no API keys. Whisper via transformers.js + Ollama / LM Studio / Jan / llamafile.

🎙️ Private transcription

Whisper runs in the browser with an automatic q8→fp32 fallback. Record the mic, a tab, or upload audio.

🧾 Every claim cited

Summaries, decisions, tasks, and chat answers link back to exact transcript moments. Owners and deadlines are never invented — if the meeting didn't say it, Scrivano doesn't claim it.

💬 Meeting Chat, strictly scoped

Ask questions about one meeting; retrieval is hard-limited to that meeting's transcript, and "not in the transcript" is an honest first-class answer.

🧠 Approved Group Memory

The AI suggests durable facts; you approve, edit, reject, or mark them temporary. Conflicts surface as cards — nothing is silently overwritten, and history is versioned.

🗂️ Groups & Group Intelligence

Meetings live in Groups with structured context. Group chat answers from active memory, documents, transcripts, and decisions — with a visible scope indicator.

🔐 Your infrastructure

Self-host the Django + Supabase backend. Workspace isolation is structural (scoped queries, tested), and external share links always expire.

"The transcript determines what happened.
Approved Group Memory explains why it matters."
— the rule every Scrivano feature obeys
